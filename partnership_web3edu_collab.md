CampusNetDAO Proposal — Web3Edu Strategic Partnership

Version: 1.0
Status: Draft

1. Summary

This proposal formalizes a collaboration between CampusNetDAO and a Web3 education partner to expand training, workshops, and onboarding programs.

2. Motivation

Education is the core pillar of CampusNetDAO. Partnering with Web3Edu organizations accelerates exposure, credibility, and hands-on learning.

3. Collaboration Areas

Co-branded workshops

Hackathons and bootcamps

Shared curriculum

Joint community projects

Mentorship funnels

4. Deliverables

3 workshops per semester

1 co-hosted hackathon

Content exchange: 5–10 modules

Dual certification badges

5. KPIs

200 students trained

30 builders retained

3–5 public outcomes (tools, demos, collabs)

6. Voting Parameters

Quorum: 10%

Approval: 60%

Duration: 48 hours
