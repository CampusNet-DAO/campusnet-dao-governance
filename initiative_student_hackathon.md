CampusNetDAO Proposal — Student Mini Hackathon Initiative

Version: 1.0
Status: Draft

1. Summary

Launch a DAO-backed student hackathon to onboard developers, designers, and innovators into the CampusNetDAO ecosystem.

2. Motivation

Hackathons accelerate ecosystem growth by bringing in passionate builders, producing usable prototypes, and fostering community culture.

3. Hackathon Structure

Theme: “Tools for the Decentralized Campus”

Duration: 48–72 hours

Tracks: governance, tooling, productivity, education

4. Prize Pool
Position	Reward
1st	80,000 CNET
2nd	40,000 CNET
3rd	20,000 CNET
Participation	5,000 CNET each
5. Judging Criteria

Innovation

Usefulness to DAO

Technical execution

Presentation

6. KPIs

50+ participants

15+ project submissions

5–7 repo-worthy tools

7. Voting Parameters

Quorum: 15%

Approval: 55%

Duration: 72 hours
