CampusNetDAO Proposal — Appoint a Community Lead

Version: 1.0
Status: Draft

1. Summary

This proposal appoints a Community Lead responsible for managing Discord operations, engagement, onboarding, and community programs.

2. Motivation

Community is the core engine of CampusNetDAO. A dedicated lead ensures consistency, structure, and growth — especially in early-stage DAO development.

3. Role Scope

Manage onboarding funnel

Run weekly community syncs

Oversee moderators

Publish monthly engagement metrics

Design campaigns for student onboarding

4. Compensation

50,000 CNET per 3-month term.
Vested monthly based on performance.
Optional: 0.05 SOL/month for admin tooling.

5. KPIs

Discord engagement up 30–50%

Weekly activity reports

200+ new members by Q2

Consistent moderation standards

6. Selection Process

Must be a verified DAO member

2-week probation

Final confirmation vote

7. Voting Parameters

Quorum: 10%

Approval: 55%

Duration: 48 hours
