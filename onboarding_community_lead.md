## 1. Proposal Summary  
This proposal seeks to officially onboard **Community Leads** across participating campuses under CampusNetDAO.  
Each lead will coordinate awareness campaigns, manage local DAO chapters, and assist in onboarding new members, ensuring the DAO scales sustainably across institutions.
